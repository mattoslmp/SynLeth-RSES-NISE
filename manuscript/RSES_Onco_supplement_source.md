## Interpretation boundaries

- Missing evidence is not negative biological evidence.
- Non-eligible domains are excluded from the eligible denominator.
- Correlation is not automatic evidence of transcriptional compensation.
- STRING combined scores are not direct experimental evidence.
- JASPAR/FIMO promoter motifs are predictions and not direct TF-binding evidence.
- Prioritized hypotheses are not discoveries unless the declared statistical and validation criteria are satisfied.
- Experimental tractability is not clinical efficacy.
